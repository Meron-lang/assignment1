KABBEE is a non-profit organization that started out of a desire to aid refugees
as they strive to transition to new lives in their adopted countries.
We believe in the motto that a little help goes a long way and that
we can make a difference in the lives of refugee communities by providing education,
technical and vocational trainings. Our vision is that we can transform
many refugees especially youth,
to become independent and future productive citizens in their adopted countries!.
